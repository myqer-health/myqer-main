About MYQER

MYQER is a patient-first emergency response QR system that shows — and speaks — the essentials when seconds matter. It works online or offline, anywhere in the world.

Mission

Help responders act faster and safer by providing clear, consent-driven information — allergies, conditions, medications, and ICE contacts — in seconds, in their language.

Why it matters

  • Clarity in chaos: emergency decisions are made fast. We surface what’s critical first.
  • Privacy by design: your data lives on your device unless you choose to share it.
  • Global by default: text + voice + translations bridge language barriers.

Safety & Privacy

  • On-device first: your card works offline; cloud features are optional.
  • Zero tracking: no analytics cookies; no hidden ads; only privacy-friendly telemetry if you opt-in.
  • Consent & control: you decide what shows on scan, and who sees what.

Compliance roadmap: GDPR baseline, HIPAA alignment for U.S. use, ISO 27001 practices as we scale.

Privacy & Trust

MYQER follows GDPR (Europe) and HIPAA (US) principles. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it. No trackers, no ads, no third-party mining.

On-Device First

Unlike cloud-only apps, MYQER works offline and caches securely. Responders can access your critical info even without signal.

From a patient, for patients

MYQER was created by someone who experienced how hard it is to be heard during emergencies. It ensures your voice and wishes are clear when you can’t speak for yourself.
