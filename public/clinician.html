Real Clinical Scenarios

Emergency Department Triage

Scenario: Unconscious patient arrives via ambulance. Family not present, no ID, language barrier with bystander.

MYQER Solution: Scan QR from phone lock screen → Instant access to allergies (Penicillin anaphylaxis), diabetes status, current medications, and ICE contact. Triage nurse has critical context in 30 seconds.

Pre-Hospital Care

Scenario: Paramedics respond to cardiac event. Patient conscious but distressed, family member present but uncertain about medications.

MYQER Solution: QR scan reveals pacemaker (2019), Warfarin therapy, recent cardiac catheterization. Paramedics adjust treatment protocol and provide accurate handover to receiving hospital.

International Travel Emergency

Scenario: Tourist collapses in foreign country. Local medical team speaks different language, no access to home medical records.

MYQER Solution: QR scan provides information in local language with voice playback. Critical allergies, chronic conditions, and emergency contacts immediately available to foreign medical team.
