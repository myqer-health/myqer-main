About MYQER

From Silence to Clarity

Emergency Medical Information at Your Fingertips

Emergencies don't wait. In those first chaotic seconds, people can't always speak for themselves — and clinicians can't guess what's unseen. MYQER was built to remove that uncertainty. With a single scan, allergies, conditions, medications, and emergency contacts become clear.

Built for Everyone

MYQER was designed to serve both the individual and the system:

👤

For People

A free, privacy-first way to carry their health essentials everywhere, even offline. No ads, no trials, no compromise. Just clarity when it matters.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

For Families

Peace of mind that their loved ones' voice will always be heard — in any language, on any phone, without needing an app installed.

🩺

For Clinicians

A triage tool that delivers consent-driven information at speed, bridging language and system barriers.

Privacy at the Core

We believe trust is built on respect. MYQER never sells data, never tracks users, and works offline by default. Information lives on your device until you choose to share it. That's not a feature — that's a promise.
