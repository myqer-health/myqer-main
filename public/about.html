About MYQER

From unknown to understood.

Emergencies don’t wait for explanations. A bystander, a partner, or a clinician may not know your allergies, conditions, or preferences when seconds matter. MYQER removes uncertainty: one scan shows or speaks your essentials in the responder’s language — so care begins without delay.

What MYQER does

  • Allergies, conditions, and medications at a glance.
  • ICE contacts responders can call instantly.
  • Care preferences that inform decisions.
  • Designed for bystanders, trusted by clinicians.

Privacy & trust

On-device first. No analytics, no ads, no tracking. You decide what shows on scan, and who sees what. GDPR-friendly and HIPAA-aware by design.
