About MYQER™

MYQER™ (pronounced “my care”) is a smart health & emergency QR Tag Aid — built to give peace of mind, anywhere, anytime.

At its core, MYQER is simple: you create a personal profile, and MYQER generates two unique QR tags:

  • Online QR → Links to your live profile, always up to date when internet is available.
  • Offline QR → Stores your vital information directly inside the code, colour-coded by triage level, ready to scan even without network.

With just one scan, responders, colleagues, or bystanders can instantly see what matters most: allergies, conditions, medications, donor status, directives, and who to call in case of emergency.

Why it matters today

Emergencies happen everywhere — at a bus stop, in the air, at school, or abroad. In those critical first minutes, MYQER removes the guesswork. It helps first responders, family, or even strangers take safe, informed action. No apps, no passwords, no subscriptions. Just a QR that tells your story when you can’t.

Why it’s free

Health and safety shouldn’t be a luxury. MYQER is designed to be accessible to everyone — children, travellers, workers, the elderly — without paywalls. It’s a public-minded tool, built to serve communities, not exploit them.

Where we’re going

MYQER is just the start. Soon, we’ll introduce:

  • Family Dashboard → manage QR tags for children, elderly parents, or loved ones.
  • Document Vault → store medical records, insurance, or travel health documents securely.
  • Global EMS Integration (MYQER+) → partner with 999, 112, and ambulance dispatchers to bridge the first five minutes of care. Dispatchers will be able to instantly pull up a patient’s QR code profile, brief ambulance crews, and cut delays in handovers to hospitals.

Our vision is for MYQER to become a universal layer of trust in emergencies — something as common as carrying ID, but smarter, more human, and built for today’s connected (and sometimes offline) world.

The promise

Whether you’re healthy or managing conditions, whether you’re travelling or simply being prepared, MYQER puts peace of mind in your pocket — and builds a safer future for everyone.
