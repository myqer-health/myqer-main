Privacy

We built MYQER to keep you safe and private — especially when you’re vulnerable.

  • On‑device first: your card works offline; cloud is optional.
  • Consent controls: you choose what appears on scan.
  • No tracking: no ads or 3rd‑party analytics; optional, privacy‑preserving telemetry only.
  • Data export/delete: export your data anytime; delete in one tap.

Compliance baseline: GDPR; HIPAA alignment for U.S. use; security best practices as we scale.
