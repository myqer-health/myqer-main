Privacy Policy

Patient‑first. On‑device by default. Zero tracking.

What we collect

Information you choose to enter into your emergency card (e.g., allergies, conditions, medicines, ICE contacts). We do not use analytics cookies and do not sell data.

Where your data lives

Your data is stored on your device. If you opt into cloud backup in the future, we’ll explain clearly and ask for consent.

Sharing

Your card is shared only when you present it (e.g., QR/wristband/phone widget). You control what is visible.

Legal basis

For users in the UK/EU, processing is based on your consent and/or vital interests during emergencies. For the US, MYQER is designed to align with HIPAA principles as applicable when integrated with covered entities.
