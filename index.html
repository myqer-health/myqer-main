Common questions

Is MYQER free?

Essential Access is free to use: your emergency card (allergies, meds, ICE), works offline, DIY printable tags, and multi‑language text + voice rolling out by language. MYQER+ is a future, optional upgrade (family profiles, clinician dashboard, premium tags, cloud backup).

Does it work without the internet?

Yes. Your emergency card is stored on your device. Screen + voice still work with no signal.

What about privacy?

No trackers, no ads. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to share it. HTTPS everywhere.

How do translations work?

We show big, clear text in the responder’s language and provide voice where available. More languages roll out progressively.

Can responders/clinicians trust the info?

The card is patient‑supplied information. We show “last updated”, and recommend cross‑checking with band/ID and confirming name/DOB with the patient or ICE where possible.

How do I start?

Create a free account, then add your essentials. You can print a tag or link a wristband/sticker any time.

New languages, tag styles, and hospital integrations are rolling out. Leave your email for early access.
